## Template Variables

- `{{COMPANY_NAME}}` - Company name
- `{{COMPANY_ADDRESS}}` - Company street address
- `{{COMPANY_CITY}}` - Company city and postal code
- `{{DATE}}` - Current date in German format (DD.MM.YYYY)
- `{{JOB_TITLE}}` - Job title from posting
- `{{OPENING_PARAGRAPH}}` - Personalized opening referencing the specific job and where you found it
- `{{WHY_COMPANY}}` - Why you're interested in this specific company (research-based)
- `{{RELEVANT_EXPERIENCE}}` - 2-3 paragraphs highlighting most relevant experience for this role
- `{{TECHNICAL_SKILLS}}` - Technical skills that match the job requirements
- `{{VALUE_PROPOSITION}}` - What unique value you bring to this role
- `{{CLOSING_PARAGRAPH}}` - Call to action and availability for interview
